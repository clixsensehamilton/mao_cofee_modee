# Phase 3: Social & Extensibility

## Objectives
- Enable social sharing and prepare the app for future expansion.
- Refine branding, gamification, and extensibility features.

## Key Features
- Platform-based sharing of coffee moods and suggestions.
- Gamification elements (badges, streaks, rewards).
- Customizable visual themes.
- Modular architecture for easy feature expansion.

## Deliverables
- Social sharing functionality integrated with major platforms.
- Gamification and theme customization options.
- Documentation for modular and extensible architecture.
- Finalized playful branding and mascot (if used).

## Verification Criteria
- Users can share moods and suggestions on social platforms.
- Gamification and theme features are available and functional.
- Architecture supports easy addition of new features.
- Branding is consistent and engaging across the app.
- All social and extensibility features are testable and functional.
