# Coffee Moodee Project Plan

## Goal
Create an engaging, AI-powered app that delivers real-time coffee suggestions based on user mood, menu photo analysis, and interactive features. The app aims to delight users with fun facts, voice-driven interaction, and social sharing, while maintaining a playful brand identity.

## User Interaction Flow
1. **Launch App**: User opens Coffee Moodee.
2. **Mood Input**: User selects or describes their mood (text or voice).
3. **Menu Photo Upload**: User uploads a photo of a coffee menu.
4. **AI Analysis**: App analyzes mood and menu photo to suggest coffee options.
5. **Suggestion Display**: AI presents coffee suggestions, fun facts, and playful commentary.
6. **Voice Interaction**: User can interact via voice for suggestions or fun facts.
7. **Sharing**: User shares their coffee mood and suggestions on platform-based social channels.

## Core Features
- **AI-Powered Coffee Suggestions**: Real-time recommendations based on mood and menu photo analysis.
- **Menu Photo Analysis**: Computer vision extracts coffee options from menu images.
- **Mood Input**: Text and voice input for mood selection.
- **Fun Facts**: Display coffee-related trivia and playful messages.
- **Platform-Based Sharing**: Share suggestions and moods on social platforms.
- **Voice Interaction**: Conversational interface for suggestions and facts.

## Enhanced Features
- **Personality & Branding**: Playful, friendly, and witty AI persona; visually engaging UI.
- **Gamification**: Mood badges, streaks, or playful rewards (optional).
- **Customizable Themes**: User-selectable visual themes (optional).

## Personality & Branding
- **Tone**: Fun, witty, and approachable.
- **Visuals**: Bright, modern, coffee-inspired design.
- **Mascot**: Optional mascot or character to reinforce brand identity.

## Technical Considerations
- **AI Models**: Natural language processing for mood, computer vision for menu analysis.
- **Voice Recognition**: Integrate speech-to-text and conversational AI.
- **Platform Integration**: Support for sharing to major social platforms.
- **Privacy**: No personalized recommendations; no user data storage beyond session.
- **Deferred Features**: Coffee shop locator and personalized recommendations are deferred.

## User Experience
- **Simple Onboarding**: Quick start, minimal setup.
- **Intuitive UI**: Easy navigation, clear prompts.
- **Responsive Feedback**: Real-time suggestions and playful responses.
- **Accessibility**: Voice and text input, readable design.

## Scalability
- **Modular Architecture**: Components for AI, UI, and sharing are loosely coupled.
- **Cloud-Based Processing**: Scalable AI and image analysis services.
- **Session-Based Data**: Stateless design for easy scaling.

## Extensibility
- **Feature Expansion**: Easy addition of new moods, coffee types, or fun facts.
- **Deferred Features**: Framework ready for future coffee shop locator and personalized recommendations.
- **API Integration**: Open for third-party plugins or integrations.

---

**Note:** Personalized recommendations and coffee shop locator are not included in the initial release and will be considered for future updates.
